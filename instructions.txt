# Instructions for Avocado Sales Demand Forecasting Project

This project involves forecasting avocado sales demand in the United States using the Triple Exponential Smoothing (Holt-Winters) method in Microsoft Excel.

## Steps Followed:

1. **Data Collection**  
   - Collected historical avocado sales data from reliable sources.  

2. **Data Cleaning**  
   - Checked for missing or inconsistent data.  
   - Formatted the data for analysis, ensuring accurate date and sales entries.

3. **Forecasting Approach**  
   - Applied Triple Exponential Smoothing to identify trends and seasonal patterns.  
   - Used Microsoft Excel's built-in formulas to apply the forecasting model.

4. **Analysis**  
   - Evaluated the forecast accuracy by comparing historical and predicted values.  
   - Visualized the results for better interpretation.

5. **Final Output**  
   - Compiled the results into a structured Excel file for easy understanding.

## How to Use the Files:

- Open `raw_data.xlsx` for the initial dataset.  
- Review `triple_exponential_smoothing.xlsx` to understand the forecasting process.  
- Analyze `final_output.xlsx` for the final forecast results.
